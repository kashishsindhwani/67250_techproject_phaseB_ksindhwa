About MonoMuse

MonoMuse fosters exploratory thinking about art and technology, offering visitors a place to encounter contemporary ideas through exhibitions, installations, and public programs. The museum brings together creative work that reflects the evolving relationship between innovation, culture, and human expression.

Through interpretation, education, and access to dynamic exhibitions, MonoMuse encourages visitors to consider how art connects to the defining questions of the present. The museum serves as both a cultural destination and a space for curiosity, inviting audiences to look closely, think critically, and discover new perspectives.

Plan Your Visit

Located in Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania, MonoMuse welcomes visitors from across the city and beyond. The museum is designed to support both guided exploration and independent discovery, offering a seamless experience from entry to exhibition spaces.

  • Open Tuesday through Sunday, 10am–5pm
  • Located in central Pittsburgh, easily accessible by public transit
  • Rotating exhibitions and interactive installations across multiple galleries
  • Public programs including talks, workshops, and special events

In addition to exhibitions, MonoMuse hosts artist talks, workshops, and evening programs that extend the experience beyond the gallery. By combining art, technology, and public engagement, the museum creates an environment that is both intellectually engaging and accessible to all visitors.
